About us

Let's get to know us because once you buy,you definitely want more!

We are a gift box business that started operating in early June 2020. Our base is in Kuala Krai, Kelantan. We provide a gift box service for customers who want to give a gift to someone special, no matter how far or close. With the tagline 'Simple Yet Worthy', we offer gift boxes for as low as RM5. We have made over 2000 gift boxes and have shipped them all over Malaysia including Sabah and Sarawak. We have also made deliveries outside Malaysia, namely in Indonesia, Brunei, and Singapore.
